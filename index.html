What is Gmail™ Cleaner?

Gmail™ Cleaner is a Google Workspace™ Add-on that crawls your entire Gmail™ mailbox and builds a detailed report of every unique sender — their name, email address, how many emails they've sent you, and when. Once you have the list, you can review it at your own pace and bulk-delete senders you no longer want to hear from.

Everything runs inside your own Google account using Google Apps Script™. No data leaves your account. All results are stored in your own Google Sheets™ spreadsheet.

How it works

  1. Install Gmail™ Cleaner from the Google Workspace™ Marketplace
  2. Open a Google Sheets™ spreadsheet and go to Extensions → Gmail™ Cleaner → Analyze Inbox
  3. The crawler runs silently in the background, building your Senders list
  4. Click Prepare Senders for Review to add a Delete/Keep dropdown to each row
  5. Mark senders at your own pace — come back whenever you like
  6. Click Process Marked Senders to delete or trash their emails

Key features

  • Crawls mailboxes of any size — tested on accounts with 36,000+ threads
  • Runs entirely in the background across multiple sessions
  • Clickable links open Gmail™ filtered to each sender for manual review
  • Move to Trash (recoverable 30 days) or Delete Permanently
  • Full audit log of every deletion
  • Safe — nothing is deleted without your explicit confirmation

Privacy & Security

Gmail™ Cleaner does not transmit, store, or share any of your data outside your Google account. All processing happens inside Google Apps Script™. Please read our full Privacy Policy for details.
